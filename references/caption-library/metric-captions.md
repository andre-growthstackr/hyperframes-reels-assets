# metric-captions

**What is this?** Caption chunks for the number reveal — the beat where a figure lands. The coral
highlight sits on the **number**, never on the surrounding words.

**When to use it?** Over `metric-card` (the 2×2 grid of big numbers) or any single-figure reveal.
Every short should carry at least one metric beat; this is its caption language.

**What feeling?** Calm authority. The number does the talking; the caption just frames it. No
exclamation, no "insane growth" — the figure is enough.

---

Format per line: chunks `[…]`, coral highlight in **bold** (always the number/figure).

1. `från` · `**flera timmar**` · `till minuter`
2. `**5 minuter.**` · `inte fem timmar`
3. `**80%**` · `mindre tid`
4. `**3x**` · `fler reels`
5. `noll` · `**redigering.**`
6. `**12 reels**` · `på en eftermiddag`
7. `en video` · `blev **fyra.**`
8. `**under 1 min**` · `per klipp`
9. `sparade` · `**6 timmar**` · `i veckan`
10. `**0 kr**` · `i redigering`
11. `samma jobb` · `**halva tiden.**`
12. `**din siffra**` · `här *(placeholder)*`
13. `från` · `**1 klipp**` · `till en kanal`
14. `**14 dagar**` · `till första reelen`
15. `**100%**` · `på autopilot`
16. `en person` · `**hela flödet.**`

## Pairing note

Pairs with `metric-card`: sync each caption chunk to the card's count-up so the number on the pill
and the number in the grid land together. Coral highlight = the figure only, mirroring the card's
`heroIndex` coral. **Never fabricate a figure** — use the script's real number or the `[din siffra]`
placeholder. If the card shows the number, the caption can carry the *unit of change* instead
(`halva tiden`, `mindre tid`) so the two don't just repeat.
