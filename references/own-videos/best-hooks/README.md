# best-hooks/

**What is this?** Your strongest openings — the first 1–3 seconds that stopped the scroll. Clips
or short notes of hooks that measurably worked.

**When to use it?** When the AI is writing or timing a hook. These are your proven opening
patterns; feed them in so the new hook rhymes with what already works for your audience.

**What feeling?** premium · calm authority · clean · digital · dark-default · coral used once.
A hook can be punchy without being loud — never meme, childish, or MrBeast-loud.
