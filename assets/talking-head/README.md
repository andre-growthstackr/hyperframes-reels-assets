# talking-head/ — reusable presenter cutaways

**What is this?** A stock of short, reusable talking-head clips of the presenter — hooks, CTAs,
reactions, and gestures (pointing up/down, nodding, thinking, surprised, a neutral listening
loop). Shot once, reused across many videos as **cutaways**.

**When to use it?** As *cutaways*, not as the spine of every video. Drop one in to react to a
metric, point the eye at a UI element, or bridge two beats — "talking head + UI + motion
graphics." A video should not be wall-to-wall talking head; these are seasoning, dropped in where
a human beat lands.

**What feeling?** premium · calm authority · clean · digital · dark-default · coral used once.
The presenter reads as calm and credible — never meme, childish, or MrBeast-loud.

## Record these clips (each 3–5s unless noted)

Shoot these once against the brand ground and reuse them everywhere:

- **Pointing up** — hand/finger pointing up (toward a caption or title above).
- **Pointing down** — pointing down (toward a CTA or "link below").
- **Nodding** — affirmative nod, agreeing.
- **Positive reaction** — a genuine positive reaction (impressed / "nice").
- **"Wait, look here"** — the attention-grab gesture that pulls the eye to the screen.
- **Neutral listening (5s)** — calm neutral loop for cutting under voiceover.
- **CTA with hand gesture (5s)** — delivering the call-to-action with a supporting hand gesture.
