# banned-caption-styles

**What is this?** The "don't" catalogue for captions — the anti-patterns that make a short read
generic, cheap, or off-brand. Each entry is a **Bra / Dåligt** contrast so the difference is
obvious. If a caption looks like something in the Dåligt column, it doesn't ship.

**When to use it?** Read this before authoring any caption, and use it as the review gate. If a
proposed caption matches a banned style, rewrite it to the Bra form.

**What feeling?** This file protects the feeling the rest of the library targets: premium, calm
authority, one coral accent. The banned styles all break that — they're loud, cluttered, or
templated. Killing them *is* the brand.

---

## 1 · Long sentences on the pill

The pill carries 1–3 words. A full sentence turns the signature pill into a subtitle bar.

- ✅ **Bra:** `rå video in` · `färdig reel ut`
- ❌ **Dåligt:** `du laddar bara upp din råa video och får en färdig reel tillbaka`

## 2 · The whole transcript on screen

Captions are chosen chunks that echo the beat, not a running dictation of everything spoken.

- ✅ **Bra:** (only the load-bearing beats captioned) `det här` · `är **sjukt.**`
- ❌ **Dåligt:** every word the voice says, word-for-word, wall to wall.

## 3 · Bottom-stuck subtitles

Captions sit on the charcoal pill at the seam (~0.54 down), never pinned to the bottom edge.

- ✅ **Bra:** pill at the seam, `seamY: 0.54`.
- ❌ **Dåligt:** white text stuck to the bottom safe-zone like auto-captions.

## 4 · TikTok rainbow words

Coral fires once per chunk, only on the load-bearing word. No per-word colour, no yellow/green/blue.

- ✅ **Bra:** `**5 minuter.**` (one coral figure, rest white)
- ❌ **Dåligt:** `5` (yellow) `minuter` (green) `inte` (blue) `timmar` (pink)

## 5 · More than 3 words per chunk

Four words is two chunks. Hard limit.

- ✅ **Bra:** `samma jobb` · `**halva tiden.**`
- ❌ **Dåligt:** `samma jobb på halva tiden nu`

## 6 · Highlighting two words in one chunk

One coral word per chunk, max. Two coral words cancel each other and break the Restraint law.

- ✅ **Bra:** `**80%**` · `mindre tid`
- ❌ **Dåligt:** `**80%** mindre **tid**`

## 7 · Hype and banned words

No `unlock`, `supercharge`, `10x`, `seamless`, `plug-and-play`, `growth hacking`, `tillväxthack`,
`revolutionera`, `game changer`. No exclamation stacking. No em-dash.

- ✅ **Bra:** `**3x**` · `fler reels`
- ❌ **Dåligt:** `revolutionera din content — 10x din tillväxt!!!`

## 8 · Emoji and decoration in the pill

The pill is bold white Inter on charcoal. No 🔥, no ✨, no arrows drawn in text.

- ✅ **Bra:** `så här` · `**funkar** det`
- ❌ **Dåligt:** `🔥 så här funkar det 👉✨`

## 9 · Tease-bait with no payoff

No "wait for it", no "you won't believe". State the thing plainly; the plainness is the hook.

- ✅ **Bra:** `det här är` · `det **snabbaste** sättet`
- ❌ **Dåligt:** `du kommer INTE tro vad som händer sen...`

## 10 · ALL CAPS shouting

Bold weight carries emphasis, not caps-lock. Sentence-case chunks.

- ✅ **Bra:** `sluta` · `göra det manuellt`
- ❌ **Dåligt:** `SLUTA GÖRA DET HÄR MANUELLT NU`

## The one-line test

> If a caption is louder than the point it makes, it's wrong. Charcoal pill, one coral word, three
> words max, at the seam. Everything else is Dåligt.
