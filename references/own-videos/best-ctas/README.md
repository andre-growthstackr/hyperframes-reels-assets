# best-ctas/

**What is this?** Your strongest closes and calls-to-action — the endings that converted (comment
for the link, book a call, follow). Clips or notes of CTAs that worked.

**When to use it?** When the AI is writing or timing the close. These are your proven CTA
patterns; feed them in so the new ending matches what already drives action.

**What feeling?** premium · calm authority · clean · digital · dark-default · coral used once —
the CTA is the one place coral often fires (a single coral pill). Never pushy, never hypey, never
MrBeast-loud.
