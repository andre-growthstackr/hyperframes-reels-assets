# Logo usage notes

Log every logo used in a render — first-party GrowthStackr marks (`brand/logos/`) and third-party
tool logos (`assets/logos/tools/`). Third-party marks are used nominatively (to identify the tool);
respect each vendor's brand guidelines and do not imply endorsement.
