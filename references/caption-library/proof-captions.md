# proof-captions

**What is this?** Caption chunks for the proof beat — before/after, "so here's what actually
happened", the moment you show the thing working. Often a denial-pivot: *this used to be hard →
now it isn't.*

**When to use it?** Over `browser-frame` and `ui-highlight` (showing the tool / dashboard / result
on screen) or `split-50-50` (head + proof side by side). The caption narrates what the viewer is
looking at, one contrast word coral.

**What feeling?** Understated proof. Calm, digital, "look, it just works." The contrast carries the
weight — *tidigare* vs *nu*, *rå* vs *färdig*. No hype adjectives.

---

Format per line: chunks `[…]`, coral highlight in **bold** (the contrast word).

1. `det här tog` · `tidigare` · `**flera timmar**`
2. `nu görs det` · `på under` · `**fem minuter**`
3. `**rå** video in` · `färdig reel ut`
4. `färdig reel ut` · `**utan** redigering`
5. `helt` · `på **autopilot**`
6. `du gör` · `**ingenting.**`
7. `samma klipp` · `**fyra** plattformar`
8. `så här` · `**funkar** det`
9. `ett klipp` · `blev **en vecka**` · `content`
10. `du laddar upp` · `resten` · `**sköts.**`
11. `inga mallar` · `ingen **timeline.**`
12. `förut` · `**för hand.**` · `nu inte`
13. `titta` · `captions **kommer** själva`
14. `zoomen` · `**sätts** automatiskt`
15. `musiken` · `**läggs** på`
16. `du ser` · `resultatet **direkt**`
17. `en render` · `**klart.**`
18. `det här är` · `**inte** en mall`

## Pairing note

Pairs with `browser-frame` / `ui-highlight`: time the coral contrast word to the moment the
`ui-highlight` box lands on the relevant part of the screen. Denial-pivot lines (`tidigare … / nu
…`) work best as two chunk-groups across a `light-flare-transition`, so the "before" reads, the
flare passes, and "nu" lands on the other side. **Truth rule holds** — if the screen shows a real
result, the caption may name it; otherwise stay generic (`resultatet`, not a made-up figure).
